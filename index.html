Know Priorities

The lost art of prioritization.

In 2011, Netflix failed to prioritize resulting in a loss of ~800K customers, >50% stock price drop, and a rating as one of the 10 most hated companies in the US.

Business Insider

Save your product, organization, and time

Prioritization is a subject that everyone has to deal with for the sake of their product, org, initiative, and even family. It can make the difference between the extremes of success and failure.

In this book you will learn

  • Secrets to what is stealing your time and success
  • Actions to improve your situation today
  • Actions to prevent or reduce future issues

Learn what is overlooked time and time again and why.
